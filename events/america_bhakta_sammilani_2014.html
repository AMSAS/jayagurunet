America Saraswata Sangha is celebrating 13th annual congregation on 3rd, 4th, and 5th day of July in year 2014 at Efland city in State of North Carolina.
 
 
 
 
 

"Bhakta Sammilani is a personification of evolution of collective feeling of the devotees. Here, 'bhaav' (divine feeling) of one individual completes the 'a-bhaav' (lack/deficit) of another. I wish, the spirits of universal brotherhood be established in the hearts of my disciples and devotees; and subsequently, find its expression in the whole of the human beings." - Swami Nigamananda
