America Saraswata Sangha is a branch of Nilachala Saraswata Sangha, Puri. Devotees obtain membership to Sangha upon their desire to love SriSriThakura, his ideology and to respect SanghaNiti (principles of Sangha).

 City, State  Names

Members have approbated SriSriThakura Nigamananda Saraswati Deva as their only Guru and Ista (diety); and asseverated to govern an ideal household life by acknowledging Jayaguru as the SiddhaMantra (hymn/intonation in Hinduism).
