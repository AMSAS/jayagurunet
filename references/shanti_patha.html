Shanti Patha
Ohm purnamadah purnamidam purnAt purnamudachyate
Purnasya purnamAdAya purnamebAbashisyate |
Guru is infinite. If you deduct infinite from the infinite, only infinite remains.
Ohm shanty shanty shanti hari ohm tat sat |
Omm, Peace, Peace, Peace. 1. Peace to the gross body, 2. Peace to the subtle body and 3. Peace to the causal body. Guru is God, Guru is Omm, Guru is Omm Tatsat. The three form of Brahman is called Omm Tatsat. 1. Brahman is devoid of qualities at the same time full of all qualities. 2. Even though the Brahman is full of qualities, He can’t be expressed and inert and formless. 3. Then again He is expressed and can take form at the request of His devotee.
Jadaksharam paribhrastam mAtrAhinancha jad bhabet
Tatsarbam kshamyatAm deba prasida parameshwara |
O compassionate Gurudev! While I was praying you I have made mistakes in uttering the prayers. Please forgive me and bestow me with your bliss.
dhyAnamulam guromurti pujamulam gurorpadam
mantramulam gurobAkyam mokshyamulam guro krupA |
Meditating upon Guru’s image the best meditation, worshipping Guru’s lotus feet is the best form of worship, Guru’s words are best of the Mantra, and salvation is Guru’s grace since only Guru can provide salvation.
twameba mAtA cha pita twameba,
twameb bandhucha sakhA twameb;
twameb bidyA drabinam twameb
twameb sarbam mama deba deba |
O Gurudev, you are my mother, you are my father, you are my friend, you are knowledge, you are in every being, and you are in every debata. I bow to you lotus feet.
