Guru Bandana
BrahmAnandam parama sukhadam kevalam jnAnamurtim
DvandAtitam gagana sadrisam tattvAmasyAdi laksyam
Ekam nityam vimalamachalam sarvadhi sAksibhutam
BhAvatitam trigunarahitam sadgurum tam namami.
He is the essence of eternal bliss, the deliverer of divine happiness, the embodiment of the highest knowledge. He is beyond earthly conflicts, all-pervading and quintessence of knowledge. He is the only eternal being, embodiment of sublime tranquility, beholder of eternal affairs. He is beyond earthly virtues yet embodiment of all virtues. He is SAT-GURU and I prostrate before His lotus-feet.
Gurbastakam
Bhaba sAgara tArana kArana hei
Rabi nandana bandhana khandana hei
SharanAgata kinkara bhitamane
Gurudeba! dayAkara dinajane |1|
O Gurudev, you are the savior of all living beings and you prevent the painful punishment of afterlife (Yama Danda). We have surrendered to you with a fearful mind. Please have mercy on your humble and indigent people.
Hruda-kandara-tAmasa-bhAskara hei
Tume Bishnu prajApati shankara hei
Param-brahma parAt-para beda bhane,
Gurudeba! dayAkara dinajane |2|
O Gurudev, you brighten the darkest corner of our heart. You are indistinguishable from the trinity (Brahma, Vishnu and Siva) since the Veda describes you as the Brahman who created the trinity. Please have mercy on your humble and indigent people.
Mana bArana-shAsana-ankusha hei
Nara trAna tare hari chAkhyusa hei
gunagAna parAyana debagane
Gurudeba! dayA kara dina jane |3|
O Gurudev, you control our minds like a goad controlling an elephant. You are the savior of human being like Hari. All the debatas always praise your excellent qualities. Please have mercy on your humble and indigent people.
Kulakundalinee-ghuma-bhanjaka hei
Hruda-granthi-bidArana-kAraka hei
Mama mAnasa chanchala rAtra dine,
Gurudeba! dayA kara dina jane |4|
O Gurudev, the kundalini awakens with your grace and you cause the breakage of the 2nd knot of illusion (Vishnu Granthi). My mind is always fickle. Please have mercy on your humble and indigent people.
Ripusudana mangala nAyaka hei
Sukha shAnty barAbhaya dAyaka hei
Traya tApa hare taba nAma gune,
Gurudeba! dayA kara dina jane |5|
O Gurudev, you are blissful and you eliminate the six lowly passions in human (lust or material needs, anger, avarice, delusion, pride and malice). You bestow happiness, peace, boon and courage. Your name eradicates the three material miseries. Please have mercy on your humble and indigent people.
abhimAna prabhAba-bimArdaka hei
gati-heena jane tume rakhaka hei
chitta shankita banchita bhakti-dhane
Gurudeba! dayA kara dina jane |6|
O Gurudev, you squash the influence of arrogance and you protect the people who can’t move. Our mind is frightened as we are devoid of devotion. Please have mercy on your humble and indigent people.
Taba nAma sadA shubha-sAdhaka hei
patitAdhama- mAnaba-pAbaka hei
mahimA taba gochara shuddhamane,
Gurudeba! dayA kara dina jane |7|
O Gurudev, your name is blissful, always bestows happiness and savior of the destitute. Only pure mind can comprehend your glory. Please have mercy on your humble and indigent people.
Jaya sadguru ishwara-prApaka hei
Bhabaroga-bikAra-binAshaka hei
Mana jenhe rahe taba shricharane,
Gurudeba! dayA kara dina jane |8|
O Gurudev, your are sat-guru and have reach the state of Brahman. You are the guiding light to people for realization of God. Victory to you. You are the cure to all malice of the material life. Please let this mind stay connected at your lotus feet. Please have mercy on your humble and indigent people.
