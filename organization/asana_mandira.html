Asana Mandira in America

America Saraswata Sangha has begun it's first Asana Mandira in USA at 1040, jayaguru Lane, Efland, NC 27243 USA. The land has been purchased on the auspicious 130th birthday of Shri Shri Thakur on 24th day of August in year 2010.



Asana Mandira Construction

Asana Mandira construction is complete. ShriShriThakura entered Mandira (temple) on july 28, 2012. The temple was named as Nigam Smruti Mandira on August 2, 2012, auspicious birthday of ShriShriThakura. More...

Bhumi Puja (Ground Breaking)

As per Hindu religion, Bhumi Puja (ground breaking) is a ceremony performed at new site before construction of home, temple or any building. Bhumi Puja has been commenced on September 11, 2010 coinciding with Ganesh Puja as per Hindu tradition in presence of members of America Saraswata Sangha.
