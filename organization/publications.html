Saraswata Publication

Over 75 years Nilachala Saraswata Sangha has been publishing books, journals on Paramahansa Srimat Swami Nigamananda Saraswati Deva in Odia, Hindi, and English. In addition, Assam-Bangiya Saraswata Matha has been publishing books in Bengali, Hindi, and English.

America Saraswata Sangha has been publishing its souvenir "Saraswata Madhuri" every year on the event of annual convocation of America Saraswata Sangha.

Nilachal Saraswat Sangha (NSS), Puri, was founded by Swami Nigamananda Paramahans Dev exclusively for his household devotees in Orissa on 24th August 1934, at Nilachal Kutir, his eternal abode at Puri, India.

Celebrates 75th year of its inception.
